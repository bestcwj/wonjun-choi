First day at Bootcamp- 5/21/2018

May 18th, 2018 was my last day at job, where saying Good-Bye to everybody was harder than I thought, I still missed few and if you happen to be one of those reading this I am sorry. Suddenly you realize you might never see them again and few might cross your path again.
