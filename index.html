Here's a time line of Dr. Choi's career life:

  • 1960 - Born in Kyungsang province, South Korea

  • 1990-1992 - Master of Medicine at Yonsei University
  • 1994-1997 - Doctor of Philosophy at Catholic University, 1996. Doctor of Medicine, Yonsei University
  • 1992.3-1996.2 - Full-time Lecturer,Department of Neurology,Yonsei University
  • 1996.3-2000.2 - Assistant Professor, Department of Neurology,Yonsei University
  • 2000.3-2002.2 - Associate Professor,Department of Neurology,Konyang University
  • 2002.3-2006.2 - Associate Professor,Department of Neurology,Yonsei University
  • 2006.3-the Present - Professor,Department of Neurology,Yonsei University
  • 2010.3-the Present - Head of the Department of Neurology,Gangnam Severance Hospital, Yonsei University

"I sharpened my skills in terms of various clinical experiences and the latest treatment techniques at the National Institute of Health(NIH) and the National Institute of Neurological Disorders & Stroke in the United States of America and I have also accumulated numerous clinical experiences according to the neural immunology, immunotherapy, and immunology, genetics, electrophysiology in muscular neurological disorders. I have abundant clinical trial results throughout the nerve-related diseases including muscle/peripheral nerves staining, molecular genetics, inflammatory neuropathy, inflammatory muscular disease, muscular dystrophy, other muscle diseases, myasthenia gravis, and multiple sclerosis, etc. Based on this, I have published excellent papers, which have been gotten good reviews, to the societies at home and abroad."
